# GDWT Helper

Train for the Geometry Dash World Tournament with accurate, real-time point tracking.

This mod adds a GDWT configuration button to the level info screen. You can set a custom time limit and point thresholds to simulate a real tournament environment. The mod automatically tracks your points based on your crashes and completions, updates a live pace HUD (Points Per Minute), and saves a detailed history of your session once the timer runs out. 

Includes an in-game Session Viewer to review your past performances and lifetime statistics.

**Note:** Practice Mode runs and 0% immediate crashes are ignored by the point tracker to prevent score inflation.
